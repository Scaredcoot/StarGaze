The meaning of the icons

  • Estimated precipitation. Precipitation like rainfall or snowfall stops you from stargazing.

  • Estimated temperature. The lower the better!

  • Estimated cloudiness. Clouds are your enemy! Our calculation is based on four different cloud factors, including altitude, density, transparency and classification.

We also calculate the stargazing status based on this factor:

    • The number of luminous sources nearby and snow/rain on the ground that reflects light. This prevents the eye to catch the stars.
